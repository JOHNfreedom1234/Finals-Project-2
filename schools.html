Our Schools

Schools of Kivotos

Kivotos has a number of schools, here we have a comprehensive list of the top schools across
the continent for your perusal. These schools all cater to a vast selection of
programs and curriculums.

Full List of Major Schools:

Trinity General School

Regarded as one of the oldest and largest of schools
Trinity General School serves as a center for peace and
theology here in Kivotos. It is home to the only religious
organization; The Sisterhood. As well numerous other clubs
such as the Justice Task Force and The Remedial Knights

Millennium Science School

Despite their fairly young history, The Millennium Science School has catapulted itself into the top thanks to their focus on Science, Engineering, and Mathematics and a focus on the development on the bleeding edge of science. With clubs like the Game Development Club and the hacker group Veritas, those who enjoy science will find themselves at home here.

Gehenna Academy

Acting as a counterpart to the peace and kindness of Trinity, Gehenna is all about causing as much chaos and and shenanigans in a short amount of time. With the vast majority of graduates becoming career criminals and mafia bosses, you'll find no shortage of scum and villainy in Gehenna. Other notable clubs include the infamous Problem Solver 68, and the Gourmet Research Club.

Abydos High School

Originally one of the largest schools in all of Kivotos Abydos High School was once the crown jewel of the desert, it's influence wide and powerful. While certain power plays and controversies have resulted in the school being reduced to a shadow of it's former self, Most of the issues have been resolved and they are now once again, accepting students.

Shanghaijiang Senior Secondary School

With their unique, chinese-inspired culture, and it's vast array of restaurants and snack bars, Shanghaijiang is one of the most famous tourist spots here in Kivotos. In addition to that, the school also holds Kivotos' best kindergarten in the form of the Plum Blossom Garden, headed by Lady Shun Sunohara.

Red Winter Federal Acadamy

Uraaaaaa! Comrade. The Red Winter Federal Acadamy is a school that takes much inspiration from the days of the Soviet Union. Located in the far nothern reaches of Kivotos. Red Winter boasts an impressive collection of weapons factories and mines. In addtiion to that, we also have the Frozen Sea Research Base, an undersea research lab dedicated to the oceanographic, and marine science research.

Valkyrie Police Academy

Serving as the main police force of Kivotos, The Valkyrie Police Academy acts to protect and serve Kivotos. Their assistance was invaluvable during times of major crises such as the Fale Sanctums incident and Kaya's Coup. Aside from that it generally deals with Public Order and Safety

Allied Hyakkiyako Academy

Another East Asian themeed academy. Hyakkiyako is also another popular destination for visitors and tourists here in Kivotos. While they may not have as much restaurants as their rival, Hyakkiyako boasts a large number of festivals and celebrations across the entirety of Kivotos.

SRT Special Academy

In cases where Valkyre cannot do the job, or the GSC needs soemthing done, the SRT Special Academy will be there to help. Serving as the Special Forces Arm of the Student Council, The SRT prides itself in being one of the best of all the schools when it comes to special operations and assymetric warfare.

Other organizations

General Student Council

The main governing body of Kivotos, the General Student Council serves as the primary body when it comes to politics that concern the entire academy city. While the schools themselves do remain indepenedent, the GSC unites and gathers all the schools under one banner. Naturally, they also remain neutral during various inter-school politics, so they also act as mediators and neutral parties.

Arius Branch School

Originally composed of extremist breakaways from Trinity led by a radical Sensei, the Arius Branch School is now once again, accepting brand new students after much of the academy was pruned. Compared to to their parent school, Trinity, Arius tends to be more practical with it's lessons and teachings, but still has the same kindness and gentleness found in every school here in Kivotos.

Federal Investigation Bureau SCHALE

An extra-governmental organization created by the Student Council President SCHALE serves as special neutral organization operating above the purview of even the student council president. A truly neutral organization that assists people in Kivotos, and in the most dire of situations, act in-stead of the GSC President. Led by the mysterious and enigmatic sensei. The organization is open to anyone who apply.
