About us

Experience. Girls. Firefights

The origins of Kivotos are unknown, but what is known is that it is a place of mystery and adventure. Filled with technological wonders and ancient ruins of a forgotten past. The academy city has three main inhabitants, the most numerous of whicha are the Students. Humans with halos above their heads. Don't let appearances fool you however, as they far stronger than any human being. Impervious to all small arms fire. Which is also the reason why firefights tend to be quite commmon across the place.

Dazzling days. Fantasy. The Academy City.

Kivotos itself is a massive city spanning thousands of kilometers across multiple lattitudes and climates. From tropical beaches and torqouise blue waters to stark tundras and expansive deserts. Kivotos, for all intents and purposes is a paradise waiting to be explored.

You. Your students. Your story together.

While we we are accepting students and visitors, what we need most are Senseis. Teachers, guardians, helpers, a beacon of light for our students to look up to in this crazy world. It will be a dangerous path, one filled with crazy adventures, glamorous sights, and journeys across the breadth of the acadamy city, but it is one that will never be forgotten no matter what.
