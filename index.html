Schools of Kivotos

Kivotos has a number of schools, here we have a comprehensive list of the top schools across
the continent for your perusal. These schools all cater to a vast selection of
programs and curriculums.

Our Top Schools:

Trinity General School

Regarded as one of the oldest and largest of schools
Trinity General School serves as a center for peace and
theology here in Kivotos. It is home to the only religious
organization; The Sisterhood. As well numerous other clubs
such as the Justice Task Force and The Remedial Knights

Millennium Science School

Despite their fairly young history, The Millennium Science
School has catapulted itself into the top thanks to their
focus on Science, Engineering, and Mathematics
and a focus on the development on the bleeding edge
of science. With clubs like the Game Development Club
and the hacker group Veritas, those who enjoy science will
find themselves at home here.

Gehenna Academy

Acting as a counterpart to the peace and kindness of Trinity,
Gehenna is all about causing as much chaos and
and shenanigans in a short amount of time. With the vast
majority of graduates becoming career criminals and mafia
bosses, you'll find no shortage of scum and villainy in
Gehenna. Other notable clubs include the infamous
Problem Solver 68, and the Gourmet Research Club.

Events and Festivals

Here in Kivotos we celebrate a number of countrywide events hosted by all schools and open to all students and visitors.

NEW YEAR'S FESTIVAL

Held in Gehenna's Hinnom Shrine, Ring in the year with a bang as we hold our Japanese inspired New Year's festivals. Kimonos, Fireworks, Wishes, Candy Apples, and Goldfish catching galore as we celebrate a New Beginning.

HALO SPORTS FESTIVAL

Hurrah! Hurrah! Fight On! A glorious once a year event where all schools compete against each other in various sporting events in the name of friendly competition and camaraderie. Naturally in Kivotos, this also has copious amounts of gunfire and explosions

Cherry Blossom Festival

Pink Petals fill the air once more as the Hyakuya Cherry Blossom begins again. Come see the famed Shizuko's Momoyodo Cafe. Hear Chise's insightful and enlightening Haikus. All under the cool spring air, and the flowering Cherry Blossoms

Other Famous Locations

Aside from the schools, Kivotos also has a number of famed Locations and Tourist Spots open to visitors. Some are friendlier than others

Fortress Eridu

A recent addition to Millennium, by President Rio Tsukatsuki, Fortress Eridu is the pinnacle of Millennium and Kivotos' technological prowess. With advanced energy weapons defensive barriers, programmable matter, and other sorts of cool technology, Eridu is the perfect place for the budding mad scientist.

Sanctum Tower

Right located at the center of Kivotos' District of Utnapishtim, The Sanctum tower serves the primary seat of power across the breadth of the academy city and the main headquarters of the student council. Towering over the city at over a kilometer tall, The Sanctum Tower is one of most recognizable and iconic parts of the Kivotos skyline, a shining beacon of light for everyone to see.

SCHALE Headquarters

Located 30 km away from the Sanctum Tower, the SCHALE office serves as the primary office of the Federal Investigation Bureau SCHALE. The extra-governmental organization led by the enigmatic Sensei. That said, the building is open to all students, and may use all of the amenities within, provided that they keep the peace.
